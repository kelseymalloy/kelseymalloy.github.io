  <h3>Ashley Fanning</h3>
  <p>
    Ashley Fanning is a Ph.D. Climatology student in UD’s Department of Geography and Spatial Sciences. Graduating in May 2025 from the University of Miami with a Bachelor of Science degree in Meteorology, she joined the research group in August 2025. Ashley’s research interests include subseasonal to seasonal prediction of climate extremes, understanding large-scale atmospheric circulation, and both chronic and acute temperature extremes. Outside of academia, Ashley enjoys hiking, running, cooking, reading, and crocheting, and she is originally from West Chester, PA.
  </p>
